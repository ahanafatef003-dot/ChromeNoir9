Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Chrome Noir ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard data when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order, we collect your name, phone number, email address, and delivery address. This information is necessary to process and deliver your order.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To send order updates and delivery notifications via SMS
  • To respond to customer service inquiries
  • To improve our website and services

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Backendless cloud infrastructure. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except where required to deliver your order (courier partners).

4. Cookies

We use localStorage (browser storage) to save your cart and orders locally on your device. No third-party tracking cookies are used on this website.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your personal data by contacting us at contact@chromenoir.co. We will process deletion requests within 7 business days.

6. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at contact@chromenoir.co
