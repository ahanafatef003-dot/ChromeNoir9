Return Policy

We accept returns within 7 days of receiving your order. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with all tags attached.

Sale items and accessories are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged.

How to Return

01

Contact Us

Email contact@chromenoir.co with your order ID, reason for return, and photos of the item.

02

Approval

Our team reviews your request within 24 hours and sends return instructions.

03

Ship Back

Pack the item securely and send it to our address. Customer bears return shipping costs.

04

Refund / Exchange

Once received and inspected, your exchange ships or refund is processed via bKash within 3 business days.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items marked SALE or FINAL SALE
  • Accessories and accessories bundles
  • Items with removed tags, signs of wear, or alterations
  • Items returned after 7 days of delivery
